A fun and engaging platform where users can share their experiences with rug pulls and meme-worthy losses. Participants earn Rug Pull Survivor NFTs or Pumpfun Badges that unlock exclusive perks.

Key Features

  • Rug Pull Stories: Share worst experiences anonymously. Top stories win special NFTs.
  • Pumpfun Integration: Exclusive NFTs, badges, and perks for Pumpfun coin holders.
  • Gamified Engagement: Redemption Quiz for badges and exclusive content.
  • Survivor's Redemption: Airdrops, voting rights, raffle entries for NFT perks.
